How to Buy BSV

If you want to buy in size (more than $20k) then you should first buy USDT and then use one of the centralized exchanges below to buy BSV


The only direct fiat onramps to BSV are the credit/debit card onramps - fees are much higher


Centralized Exchanges


  • OKX*
  • KuCoin*
  • Gateio*
  • MEXC
  • XT.com
  • Coinex

Non-KYC Swaps


  • Changelly (email sign-up)
  • Houdiniswap
  • ChangeHero
  • Exodus

USA Onramps


  • Rockwallet*

Credit/Debit Card Onramps


  • Handcash*
  • BuyBSV.com*
  • Centbee*



*KYC required. Non-asterisk exchanges/swaps do not require KYC under certain limits, varies between exchange
